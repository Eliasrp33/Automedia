Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Automedia (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Automedia website and services (the “Service”).

Information we collect

  • Account information: email address and login credentials you provide.
  • Content you upload: photos/videos you upload for generating captions or posting.
  • Generated content: AI-generated titles, captions, and hashtags.
  • Connected account tokens: when you connect social accounts (e.g., TikTok), we store access tokens on our backend to post on your behalf.
  • Usage and diagnostics: basic logs needed to operate and secure the Service.

How we use information

  • Provide the Service (upload media, generate content, publish posts).
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Improve reliability, prevent abuse, and debug issues.

How we share information

  • AI providers: uploaded images may be sent to an AI provider to generate text output.
  • Social platforms: when you publish, we send your media and caption to the platform you chose.
  • Legal: we may disclose information if required by law.

Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide the Service. You can request deletion of your account and stored tokens by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Your choices

  • You can disconnect connected social accounts in the platform settings (and we can revoke tokens).
  • You can request deletion of your account data.
